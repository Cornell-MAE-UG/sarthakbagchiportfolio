## About Me

I am a third year mechanical engineering student, currently interested in propulsion systems. As a member of Cornell's Liquid Propulsion team, I contributed to the design and development of a 500 N liquid bipropellant engine, including the design of an augmented spark igniter. I enjoy applying principles of fluid mechanics, heat transfer, and thermodynamics alongside mechanical design, using computational fluid dynamics to analyze engineering systems. Explore my projects to see my work in more detail!
